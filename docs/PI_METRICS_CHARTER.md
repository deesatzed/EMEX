# PI Metrics Charter

Phase 1 measures process readiness and shadow-evaluation readiness, not clinical effectiveness.

## Initial Metrics

- Redaction completion before OE packet copy.
- Post-T0 leakage blocked.
- OE output parsed into clinician-draft structure.
- Missing information surfaced rather than forced.
- Suggestions labeled as clinician draft.
- Artifact completeness for each run.

## Later Metrics

- Order concordance.
- Delayed concordance.
- Potential order lead time.
- Potential result lead time.
- Calibration by risk bucket.
- Cost/restraint cautions.
- Unknown outcome as safety debt.

These later metrics require approved real-world data and are not established by the synthetic demo.
